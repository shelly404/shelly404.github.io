Orange Cipher

Quantum resistant, mathematically unbreakable , and simple enough that even an orangutan could use it!

Meet Orange Cipher . It's based of off the only cipher that's theoretically unbreakable: the one-time pad cipher. Most modern encryption relies on primes and favorable factoring odds (which quantum computers will quickly render obsolete), but Orange Cipher uses good-old fashioned keys. Do you want real, quantum-resistant security? You should check out Orange Cipher.
